# Login to Vercel via Browser

## Step 1: Go to Vercel Website
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://vercel.com**
3. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Log In"** (top right)

## Step 2: Create Account or Login
- **Option A:** Sign up with GitHub (recommended)
- **Option B:** Sign up with Google
- **Option C:** Sign up with Email

## Step 3: Verify You're Logged In
- You should see the Vercel dashboard
- Your email/name should be in the top right corner

## Step 4: Come Back Here
Once you're logged in to Vercel in your browser, tell me and we'll continue!
