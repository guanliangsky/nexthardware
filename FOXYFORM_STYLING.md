# 🎨 FoxyForm Styling Guide

## Recommended Settings to Match Your Site

Based on your site's design, here are the **exact values** to use in FoxyForm:

### ✅ **Recommended Settings:**

```
Background Color: #ffffff
Font Color: #1a1a1a
Font: Arial (or Inter if available)
Font Size: normal
```

### 📋 **Detailed Breakdown:**

#### **Background Color:**
- **Value:** `#ffffff` ✅ (You already have this correct!)
- **Why:** Your contact section uses `bg-white` class

#### **Font Color:**
- **Value:** `#1a1a1a` (instead of `#000000`)
- **Why:** Your site uses `text-slate-900` which is `#0f172a` or `#1a1a1a` - a softer black that's easier on the eyes
- **Alternative:** If `#1a1a1a` isn't available, use `#0f172a` or `#000000` (pure black works too)

#### **Font:**
- **Value:** `Arial` ✅ (You already have this correct!)
- **Why:** Arial is very similar to Inter (your site's font) - both are clean sans-serif fonts
- **Alternative:** If FoxyForm offers "Inter" or "System UI", use that instead

#### **Font Size:**
- **Value:** `normal` ✅ (You already have this correct!)
- **Why:** Matches your site's default text size

### 🎯 **Your Site's Actual Colors:**

From your code:
- **Background:** `#ffffff` (white)
- **Headings:** `#0f172a` or `#1a1a1a` (slate-900)
- **Body Text:** `#475569` (slate-600)
- **Font:** Inter (sans-serif)

### 💡 **Pro Tips:**

1. **If FoxyForm has a "Border Color" option:**
   - Use: `#e2e8f0` (slate-300) to match your form inputs

2. **If FoxyForm has a "Button Color" option:**
   - Use: `#0f172a` (slate-900) to match your site's buttons

3. **If FoxyForm has a "Button Text Color" option:**
   - Use: `#ffffff` (white)

### ✅ **Final Recommended Settings:**

```
Background Color: #ffffff
Font Color: #1a1a1a (or #0f172a)
Font: Arial
Font Size: normal
```

These settings will make your FoxyForm blend seamlessly with your contact section! 🎨
